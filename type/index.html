Filter by Type

There are three "types" on this list: anthropomorphic, zoomorphic, and realistic. Anthropomorphism means that prominent animal characters in the game are mostly animal with some human features. Zoomorphism means that prominent animal characters in the game are mostly human with some animal features. Realism means that prominent animal characters in the game have no human traits.
